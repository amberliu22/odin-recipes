Matcha

Description

This is a recipe of Amber's favorite matcha that she has every morning. Use this recipe for a seamless, stress-free way to start your weekdays.

Ingredients

  • ceremonial matcha powder
  • hot water, boiled to 175 F

    • Steps

    1. Boil water to 175 F
    2. Get a teaspoon of matcha powder and pass it through a sift into your cup
    3. Pour 1/4 of the water into the cup
    4. Use a bamboo or electric whisk to mix the matcha powder and water, until the top layer gets slightly foamy
    5. Pour the rest of the water into the cup. That's it!
